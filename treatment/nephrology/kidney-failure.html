KIDNEY FAILURE? KIDNEYS NOT FUNCTIONING?

AKI ( ACUTE KIDNEY INJURY)
Some patients may have sudden acute shut down of the kidney functions due to a number of causes. For example after post poisoning, post snake bite, drug toxic effects or sudden fall of blood pressure following bleeding due to any reason or accidents, surgery etc., These patients will require immediate dialysis treatment after appropriate investigations.
Treatment : HEMODIALYSIS
