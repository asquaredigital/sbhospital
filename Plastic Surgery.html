Many patients with Urological problems and also non urological problems require reconstructive surgery when there is loss of tissues or defects in body parts. Well experienced and well qualified Plastic surgeons carry out such treatments at SB hospital. Patients with uncontrolled Diabetes and with narrowing of blood vessels end up with damage to tissues or parts of the limbs. Removal of such damaged or dead tissues and reconstruction of the that part so that normal or at least near normal status is restored so that these patients can return to their normal activities and livelihood. Occasionally children are born with defects in the face or external genetary, limbs and correction or reconstruction of the affected part is done by the Urological Surgeon of the SB Hospital or plastic surgeon or by the pediatric surgeon who are in the panel of SB Hospital.
