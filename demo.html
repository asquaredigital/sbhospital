Started with a passion for Providing a Centre of Excellence for health care In Select Super Specialities Particularly for the People who cannot afford the exorbitant expenses In Corporate Hospital

The chairman of the hospital was one of the Senior most Urologists and andrologist had Initially Started the Institution with above motto to render Service In The Field of Genito Urinary Surgery, Male Infertility and for the Treatment of Sexual Problems in the Male from the year 1990..

In View Of The Persistent Demand From The Patients, and also to Provide total Complete Care full fledged Centre for treatment of Infertile Couples, both male and Female, was Started with most modern Techniques in the field of assisted reproductive technology therapies.

Since Then, with a far reaching outlook and Vision, It Has been ever Expanding Developments In Various Fields at SB Hospital.

In View of the rapidly increasing incidence of Diabetes Mellitus and Cardiac illness It was decided to Provide Ultra Modern Facilities for Treatment of such Patients. Also, at the request of our old patients and their relatives, and to avoid our patients going around for treatment of other illnesses, specialities such as Paediatrics, Paediatric surgery, Plastic Surgery were also added.
