UROLOGY, NEPHROLOGY, RENAL TRANSPLANTATION SERVICES

The first super speciality hospital in the year 1990 to offer a complete urology, nephrology & renal replacement therapy UNDER ONE ROOF.
At SB Hospital, we have one of the best dialysis unit. There are 10 dialysis units and separate units for HCV positive patients. Very high standards are maintained under the supervision of well qualified and well experienced Nephrologist.
Haemodialysis can be done simultaneously for twelve ( ten routine and two for Hcv)patients. A separate dialysis machine is available in the ICU also so that a critically ill patient in the ICU can have dialysis in the ICU if and when required without shifting
Renal transplantation services was started from 2011
Thanjavur SB hospital has been approved for government employees and also for most of the insurance companies

SB CARDIO VASCULAR CENTRE

Cardiology & Cardiothoracic Surgery Was Started In The Year 2020 Persistent demand from the patients coming for treatment of urological and nephrological patients and their relations that being a well developed super speciality hospital rendering good service to them prompted to start facilities for rendering similar service to patients with cardiac and other ailments. Therefore, the services of a good team of cardiologist and his team with cardiac technician was enlisted. Advanced technically safe and efficient equipment were acquired. Within a short time of three months, a number of coronary angiograms and angioplasties were performed. This rendered the necessity of follow up surgical procedures for those not fit for coronary angioplasties. Well experienced and smart team of cardiothoracic surgical team was soon involved in starting the cardio thoracic surgical department.
