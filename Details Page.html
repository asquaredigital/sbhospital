TREATMENT FOR URINARY TRACT STONE

EXTRACORPOREAL SHOCK WAVE LITHOTRIPSY

S.B Hospital has the Longest experience with this high tech successful treatment for kidney stones. With an advanced Dorniere machine ( ultrasound and Fluroscopy) guided, shockwaves are sent in to the urinary tract, focussed on to the stone and the stones are broken in to small pieces which are usually passed out in the urine. usually this stone breaking procedure is done without anaesthesia and in such cases there will be no need for admision in to the hospital.
